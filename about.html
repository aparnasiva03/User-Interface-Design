About the Events

We specialize in hosting a wide range of events, from corporate conferences and meetings to weddings and social gatherings. With our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated staff, we ensure that every event is a memorable and successful one.

Our experienced event planners work closely with our clients to understand their unique requirements and create customized event packages that cater to their specific needs. Whether you're planning a small intimate gathering or a large-scale event, we have the expertise and resources to make it a grand success.

From elegant ballrooms and conference halls to outdoor spaces with breathtaking views, our hotel offers versatile event venues to suit any occasion. Our professional staff is committed to delivering exceptional service, ensuring that every aspect of your event is meticulously planned and executed.

Majestic Ballrooms

The Majestic Ballroom provides a magnificent backdrop for weddings, receptions, gala events and corporate meetings. Situated on the second floor and flooded with natural light, it is directly accessible from the main lobby and car park by separate lifts as well as escalators. One of the largest pillar-less function spaces in Kuala Lumpur, it seats 1,200 for a banquet or 1,500 theatre-style.

We work closely with you to create bespoke weddings for that special day in your life. In this elegant environment with dramatic chandeliers, plush carpeting and a built-in stage, the bride and groom will surely sparkle as they make their way up the aisle.

Majestic Wing Boardrooms

The Boardroom in the Majestic Wing evokes the nostalgia of the colonial era. Dark timber floors, a touch of art décor and framed photographs of old Malaya all contribute to the warm ambience of this private meeting room. Comfortable seating for twelve persons surrounds the boardroom table and butler service is provided to cater to your every need. A set luncheon is served in the Colonial Café of this historic hotel, carrying through the feel of yesteryear.

Meeting Rooms

The 13 meeting rooms suit small corporate events and social functions ranging in size from 20 to 120 guests. The elegance of the rooms is enhanced by natural light and complemented by the hotel’s culinary expertise. Coffee breaks or pre-dinner cocktails may be served in the foyers of each of the two floors of function rooms. Ideal for meetings, brainstorming sessions, business lunches or intimate dinners, the meeting rooms are easily accessed by a separate lift from the main lobby and car park.

Roof Garden

The Roof Garden at The Majestic provides a unique outdoor setting for celebrating wedding receptions as well as hosting private parties and elegant corporate events. Intimate, stylish and romantic, it has the flexibility to accommodate just about every type of event, from business soirees to private marriage ceremonies. The Roof Garden captures the intimacy and charm that a standard ballroom or function room simply cannot offer. No other spot can boast of a better view of Kuala Lumpur’s famous historical mile.
